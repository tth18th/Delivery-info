Onboarding

First Steps

  • Complete your background check & drug test.
  • Attend the orientation session (in-person or virtual).
  • Set up your Amazon Flex / DSP app credentials.
  • Watch safety & delivery training videos.
  • Get your uniform, badge, and device.
Pro tip: Take notes during orientation — small details save time later.

Must-Know Policies

  • Delivery time windows & priority packages.
  • Customer notes & access codes.
  • Return & undeliverable procedures.
  • Safety protocols (seatbelt, handbrake, etc.).
  • App navigation & scanning basics.
Remember: Always log out of the app when not in use.

Morning Routine

Pre-Departure

  • Arrive at the station 15–20 min early.
  • Hydrate & eat a light breakfast.
  • Check your schedule & route map.
  • Inspect your device & portable charger.

App Check

  • Sign in to the delivery app.
  • Verify your route & stop count.
  • Download offline maps (if available).
  • Check for any route updates.

Mindset & Safety

  • Stretch your neck, shoulders, and back.
  • Review the day's weather forecast.
  • Set a positive intention for the shift.
  • Ensure your phone is fully charged.
Pro: A 5-min stretch reduces fatigue by 30%.

Van Loading

Smart Loading

  • Sort packages by stop order (use the driver aid number).
  • Place first stops near the side/back doors.
  • Heavy items on the bottom, light on top.
  • Use shelves & totes to separate zones.
  • Keep a clear path to the cargo area.

Scan & Verify

  • Scan each package during loading.
  • Check for damaged or missing labels.
  • Note any oversized or special-handling boxes.
  • Double-check the total package count.
Warning: Unscanned packages cause delays — scan every single one.

Loading Efficiency Checklist

  • Group by zone (A, B, C, D) or stop number.
  • Place envelopes & small boxes in the front seat (safe zone).
  • Use bungee cords or nets to secure tall stacks.
  • Keep the handbrake & gear shift area clear.

During Delivery

At Each Stop

  • Park safely & engage the handbrake.
  • Grab the correct package (check label).
  • Follow customer notes (gate codes, etc.).
  • Take a clear photo of the delivery.
  • Mark as delivered in the app.

Time Management

  • Keep a steady pace — don't rush, don't lag.
  • Use the app's route optimization.
  • Group nearby stops to save time.
  • If stuck, skip and come back later.

Customer Interaction

  • Be polite and professional.
  • Hand packages with care.
  • If the customer is unavailable, follow the return procedure.
  • Leave packages in a safe, dry spot.
Golden rule: Treat every package like it's your own.

Road Issues

Vehicle Problems

  • Flat tire? Call roadside assistance immediately.
  • Engine warning light — pull over safely and report.
  • Low fuel — plan your refueling stops.
  • Brake issues — do not drive; call for help.

Navigation & Traffic

  • Use the app's reroute feature for delays.
  • Check traffic updates before each leg.
  • If a road is closed, find an alternate route.
  • Stay calm in heavy traffic — safety first.
Emergency: Save dispatch & support numbers in your phone.

What to Do in an Accident

  • Check for injuries — call 911 if needed.
  • Move the van to a safe spot if possible.
  • Report the incident to dispatch immediately.
  • Take photos of the scene and damage.
  • Complete the incident report in the app.

End Routes

Returns & Undelivered

  • Scan all undelivered packages as "return."
  • Sort returns by reason (access, no response, etc.).
  • Place returns in designated totes/bins.
  • Log each return in the app.

Final App Steps

  • Complete all required app prompts.
  • Submit your delivery completion report.
  • Check for any pending customer feedback.
  • Sign out of the delivery app.

Wind-Down

  • Debrief with your dispatcher (if applicable).
  • Note any issues or route improvements.
  • Hydrate and have a snack.
  • Prepare for the next shift.
Reflect: What went well? What could be better?

Van Check (End of Day)

Inspection Checklist

  • Remove all personal items and trash.
  • Check for any packages left behind.
  • Inspect for damage (scuffs, dents, glass).
  • Test lights, horn, and indicators.
  • Check tire pressure and fluid levels.

Refuel & Clean

  • Refuel if below ¼ tank (DSP policy).
  • Wipe down high-touch surfaces (steering wheel, door).
  • Vacuum or sweep the cargo area.
  • Report any maintenance issues.
Final step: Take a photo of the van odometer and fuel level.

Daily Maintenance Log

  • Record mileage & fuel used.
  • Note any mechanical concerns.
  • Log cleaning & sanitization.
  • Submit the van check report.
